Our Heritage

A journey of tradition, family, and the relentless pursuit of authentic Persian sweetness

From Tehran to Sydney

Saffran Patisserie was born from a deep love for Persian pastry artistry and a desire to bring authentic, handcrafted delicacies to the Hills District. Our journey began in 1987 in Tehran, where our family first started crafting traditional Persian sweets using recipes passed down through generations.

When we moved to Sydney, we brought with us not just our recipes, but our passion for the intricate art of Persian dessert making. Every morning, our kitchen comes alive with the careful measuring of saffron, the gentle folding of dough, and the patient waiting for pastries to achieve perfection.

From the delicate layers of our Persian baklava to the aromatic spices in our traditional cookies, every creation that leaves our kitchen represents our commitment to preserving the rich culinary heritage of Persia while sharing it with our Australian community.
