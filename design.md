# Saffran Patisserie Website Design Guide - Updated

## Design Philosophy

**Persian Heritage with Modern Elegance**
- Authentic representation of Persian/Middle Eastern pastry culture
- Warm, inviting colors inspired by saffron, rose water, and Persian ceramics
- Clean, sophisticated aesthetic that honors the 1987 family heritage
- Focus on the artisanal quality and traditional techniques

## Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**
- Saffron Gold: #F4A460 (primary accent, representing saffron)
- Persian Rose: #D4A5A5 (secondary accent, representing rose water)
- Warm Cream: #F8F6F0 (background)
- Deep Teal: #2F4F4F (text and accents)

**Supporting Colors:**
- Pure White: #FFFFFF (cards/containers)
- Light Grey: #F5F5F5 (subtle backgrounds)
- Warm Brown: #8B4513 (earth tones)

## Typography

**Display Font:** Playfair Display (serif) - for headings and elegant emphasis
**Body Font:** Inter (sans-serif) - for readability and modern feel
**Accent Font:** Dancing Script (script) - for special decorative elements

## Visual Language

**Imagery Style:**
- High-quality, naturally lit photography
- Focus on traditional Persian ingredients: saffron, pistachios, rose water
- Authentic presentation with Middle Eastern styling
- Warm, inviting atmosphere with cultural elements

**Layout Principles:**
- Grid-based alignment
- Generous white space
- Content hierarchy through typography scale
- Mobile-first responsive design

## Visual Effects & Animation

**Libraries Used:**
- Anime.js: Smooth micro-interactions and element reveals
- Typed.js: Elegant text typewriter effects
- Splide: Image carousels and galleries
- ECharts.js: Data visualization for business insights
- p5.js: Creative background effects
- Leaflet: Interactive maps

**Effect Implementation:**
- Subtle fade-in animations on scroll
- Gentle hover transitions
- Typewriter effect for hero headlines
- Infinite image scrollers for product showcases
- Soft background particle effects

**Header Effect:**
- Clean navigation with subtle shadow
- Smooth color transitions
- Mobile-optimized hamburger menu

## Interaction Design

**User Experience:**
- Intuitive navigation flow
- Quick access to ordering platforms
- Visual menu exploration
- Location and contact prominence
- Social media integration

**Interactive Elements:**
- Product image galleries
- Hover-activated details
- Smooth page transitions
- Touch-friendly mobile interactions
