Crafted with Heritage

Saffran Patisserie brings the authentic taste of Persian and Middle Eastern sweets to Baulkham Hills. Our family has been perfecting these recipes since 1987, when we first started our journey in Tehran. Today, we continue that tradition in Sydney, using the same time-honored techniques and premium ingredients.

From the delicate layers of our Persian baklava to the aromatic spices in our traditional cookies, every creation represents our commitment to preserving the rich culinary heritage of Persia while sharing it with our Australian community.
